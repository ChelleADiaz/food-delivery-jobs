Plannig To Join A Delivery Service?

The food delivery industry has created numerous food delivery jobs. This has turned into an instant source of income for many individuals around the world. Food delivery jobs can be suitable for any lifestyle; whether you already have a full time job and are just looking for some extra money, or a University student saving up for tuition. The question is, which food delivery platform is right for you?

Although food delivery jobs have many things in common, there are also notable differences. The following information will be a comparison between four different food delivery jobs located in Edmonton. We will be looking at: SkipTheDishes, DoorDash, UberEats and InstaCart.

What are the requirements before starting? How much do people get paid? How are hours setup?

Food Delivery Opportunity #1: SkipTheDishes

  • Have a fully functional vehicle
  • Proof of a valid driver’s license
  • Valid vehicle insurance & registration
  • Background check required
  • Approved Thermal Bags [$59.95 required]
  • Good phone and data plan
  • Documentation stating that you are legally allowed to work (Passport, Work Permit)

Skip Earnings

As a Skip Courier, individuals earn money with delivery fees and customer tips. You should also consider the number of deliveries you make, the distance you drive during a shift. When it comes to cash orders, you will receive payments immediately.Online order payments will be deposited in your account weekly. Hours are flexible, depending on your availability, but must be scheduled in the app.

Skip orders consist of prepared fast food and restaurant orders.

Food Delivery Opportunity #2: DoorDash

  • An individual must have access to a smartphone (iPhone or Android)
  • Proof of valid license and insurance
  • Must be 18 or over
  • Required to have 1 or more years of driving experience
  • Verification of identity (SIN, fill out a W9 form)
  • Background check required

DoorDash Earnings

DoorDash has developed an equation to help calculate pay for drivers. Base Pay (Time, Distance, Desirability) + Promotions (Peak Pay, Challenges) + Tips = Total Earnings. Hours are flexible, it is all up to YOU, just log on to the app.

Food Delivery Earnings Definitions

Base pay is the starting point for each DoorDash driver. This amount may vary, starting at $2 up to $10 or more. Depending on your situation (Time, Distance, Desirability of specific order). If the order is located at a further location that is not well known, your base pay will increase.

Peak pay will only be collected on busy days, this will automatically be added to your pay if you have shifts on those days.

Challenges are little forms of encouragement, so that drivers I'm more motivated to acquire more deliveries. For instance, If a driver picks up more orders drawing a specific time frame they can earn more money.

DoorDash is available for restaurant orders

>Food Delivery Opportunity #3: UberEats

UberEats is a unique food delivery job because it allows different forms of transportation, depending on which city you are located in. UberEats does not only offer fast food and restaurant orders, it also has grocery delivery.

Requirements when driving a vehicle:

  • Valid Driver’s License (Specific Class: Class 5, G etc.)
  • Your vehicle must qualify in the approved standards (2 door, or 4 door that is 20 years old or newer)
  • You must be 21+
  • Valid vehicle registration and insurance
  • Proof of permission to legally work ( Canadian Passport, Study or Work permit, Canadian Birth Certificate, Permanent Resident or Citizenship Card)
  • Background checks (Criminal and Driving Evaluations)
  • Good phone and data plan

Requirements for other sources of transportation:

  • Requirements for other sources of transportation:
  • You must be 18 or 19 years old (province regulations)
  • Proof of permission to legally work ( Canadian Passport, Study or Work permit, Canadian Birth Certificate, Permanent Resident or Citizenship Card)
  • Background checks (Criminal and Driving Evaluations
  • Good phone and data plan

Earnings

When it comes to UberEats earnings, there are set rates:

  • $1.50 per order pick up
  • $0.46 per kilometer
  • $0.19 per minute
  • $1.00 per drop off
  • Boosts are also offered during high demand times

The Uber Fee or Commission is deducted from your fare. This may vary from 5-35%.
